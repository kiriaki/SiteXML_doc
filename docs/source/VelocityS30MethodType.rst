VelocityS30MethodType
=====================

**Type:** ``simpleType``
**Description:**
  Method used for the extraction of S-wave velocity profiles and, thus, 
  				of the average shear-wave velocity over the top 30 meters of the soil column, Vs30.
  				Used to determine the Quality Index of the provided Vs30 of a site.
  				For more information, see SERA Deliverable 7.1, Appendix IV.

Possible Values
--------------

* ``Geology`` – * ``Topographic Slope`` – * ``SPT`` – * ``CPT`` – * ``Laboratory`` – * ``S-REFR`` – * ``S-REFL`` – * ``SASW`` – * ``MASW`` – * ``SWI`` – * ``SPAC/F-K`` – * ``ReMi`` – * ``Crosshole`` – * ``Downhole`` – * ``Uphole`` – * ``P-S Log`` – * ``Seismic Cone`` – * ``DH Strong Motion Arrays`` –
